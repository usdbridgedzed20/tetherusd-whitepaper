Tether USD Bridged ZED20 - Whitepaper

1. Introduction

Tether USD Bridged ZED20 is a decentralized token deployed on the BEP-20 network. Its primary purpose is to replicate the experience and functionalities of a standard USDT token with unique features for bridge operations and swap compatibility.

2. Token Specifications

  • Name: Tether USD Bridged ZED20
  • Symbol: USDT.z
  • Decimals: 18
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000
  • Network: Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Contract: 0x3045f703b5a61a065186c4008469c426c5b2d2b0

3. Purpose and Use Case

The token is designed for swappable operations, flash tether transfers, and to act as a mirrored stablecoin on the BEP-20 chain. It's compatible with major wallets like Trust Wallet and supports cold wallet visibility.

4. Token Distribution

100% of the supply is managed centrally and may be distributed through controlled mechanisms including mint, freeze, blacklist, and reserve strategies.

5. Disclaimer

This token is not affiliated with the original Tether (USDT) project. It is independently developed for experimental, educational, and bridge utility purposes.
